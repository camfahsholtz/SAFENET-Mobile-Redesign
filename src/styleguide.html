Narrative

At approximately 1430 on 04/14/2026, crew was operating on Division Bravo near the drainage bottom when primary repeater on Pine Mountain went offline. Command net traffic ceased for all units operating below the ridge. Attempted to contact Division via IAP-listed alternate tac channel; no response. Crew observed significant fire activity upslope with no ability to transmit to Division Supervisor or adjacent Division. Duration of outage approximately 4 hours 15 minutes. Workaround attempted via secondary tac frequency; partial contact with adjacent crew but no command-level communication restored until repeater was swapped at approximately 1845.

Immediate Action Taken

Crew boss made decision to pull crew back to the previous safety zone at 1515 after third failed attempt to reach Division. Posted lookouts on high ground with hand signals to maintain visual contact with adjacent crew. Used cell phone to reach dispatch directly at 1540 to report condition and request repeater check. Held position in safety zone until 1845 when repeater was restored and verbal contact with Division reestablished.
